Intelligent Decision Support Systems (IDSS) have become essential components in modern decision-making processes, assisting humans in domains such as healthcare, finance, logistics, education, and governance. As these systems increasingly rely on AI, machine learning, and computational intelligence, ensuring fairness, transparency, and trustworthiness has emerged as a critical research priority.

Traditional approaches emphasize accuracy and efficiency, but often ignore crucial aspects such as:

  • Ethical reasoning
  • Interpretability
  • Management of uncertainty

This special session provides a multidisciplinary platform for the IEEE fuzzy systems community to explore theoretical advances, methodological developments, and practical applications addressing fairness and trustworthiness in intelligent decision support.

Fuzzy logic and soft computing paradigms offer powerful mechanisms to support decisions that are explainable and aligned with human reasoning.

By integrating fuzzy systems with modern AI, machine learning, and data-driven IDSS models, researchers can design hybrid approaches that enhance:

  • Interpretability
  • Ethical accountability
  • Fair decision-making
